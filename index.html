The Product Engine

How I turn complex business problems into elegant, validated product realities.

01

Decode

I don't start with pixels; I start with the truth. Using SQL, Tableau, and MECE problem-solving, I strip away assumptions to uncover what users are actually doing versus what they say they are doing.

02

Align

Once the problem is isolated, I build the blueprint. I map out user journeys and ruthlessly prioritize feature sets using RICE and KANO frameworks to ensure we build for measurable impact, not just output.

03

Materialize

Strategy means nothing if it can't be executed. I translate PRDs into high-fidelity Figma prototypes and hand off clean, agile roadmaps that bridge the gap between design and technical feasibility.
